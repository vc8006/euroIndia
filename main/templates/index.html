About Us

The founder & director of EURO INDIA is a Czech national with international experience in Germany and India.

  • Simona Jadronova studied Economics with emphasis on developing countries, international law and marketing at the TU Bergakademie Freiberg, Germany.
  • Ms Jadronova's work experience began in Germany at several social and politic institutions and marketing agencies.
  • In India, she worked at the Indo-German Chamber of Commerce, where she supported and helped with the incorporation of reputed German companies-being a key player in creating their processes and business development. Her advisory role has enabled many companies to succeed in the Indian markets.
